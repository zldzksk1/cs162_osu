The necessity of training farm hands for first class farms in the fatherly handling of farm live stock is foremost in the eyes of farm owners. 

Since the forefathers of the farm owners trained the farm hands for first class farms in the fatherly handling of farm livestock, 

the farm owners feel they should carry on with the family radition of training farm hands of first class farmers in the fatherly handling of farm live stock 

because they believe it is the basis of good fundamental farm management.
